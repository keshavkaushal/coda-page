A piano app for everyone

Coda is a piano MIDI player that creates falling notes to show you which keys to play.

Coda plays MIDI files, so you can learn ANY song from my YouTube channel! MIDI files for all of my 200+ covers are available for free.

Features

The next keys to play will light up in red so you can find the right notes easily.

Useful controls to help you navigate through the song. For example, you can set a bookmark at a specific point. Whenever you want to rewind to that part, just tap once on the left-hand side of the screen.

Adjust the song tempo until you're comfortable to follow along. You can slow down the song to 0.1x with no loss in quality and an accurate piano sound sampled from a Yamaha C5 grand piano.

Coda runs in your web browser, so you don't need to install any apps or software. Android, iOS, Windows and Mac are all supported!
