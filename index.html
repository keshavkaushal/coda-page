Overview

Coda is a piano MIDI player that creates falling notes to show you which keys to play next.

Learn my Covers

You can learn ANY song from my YouTube channel! MIDI files for all 200+ videos are available for free.

In addition, piano MIDI files are available online for almost every popular song. Just search for "Song Name piano MIDI"

Light-up Keys

In Coda, the next keys to play will light up in red so you know exactly where to place your fingers. Finding chords just became so much easier!

Easy Song Navigation

Coda has useful controls to help you navigate through any song. For example, you can set a bookmark at a specific point. Whenever you want to rewind to that part, just tap once on the left-hand side of the screen.

Compatible Everywhere

Coda runs in your web browser, so you don't need to install any apps or software. This also means it can run on any device. Android, iOS, Windows and Mac are all supported!

Learn at your own speed

Adjust the song tempo until you're comfortable to follow along. You can slow down the song to 0.1x with no loss in quality and an accurate piano sound sampled from a Yamaha C5 grand piano.

Great Value

Coda offers all of the features needed to learn new songs on the piano for a fraction of the price of other piano apps (Simply Piano is $149.99 per year! 🤢). This makes it the ideal choice for the pianist who wants to learn new songs.
